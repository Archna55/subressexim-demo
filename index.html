🌾 Our Product Categories

At SUBRESS EXIM, we offer a wide range of carefully selected products sourced from trusted farmers and manufacturers.

🌶️ Indian Spices

India is known worldwide for its rich and aromatic spices. We supply premium quality spices that add authentic flavor and aroma to every dish.

Examples:

Turmeric, Red Chilli, Cumin Seeds, Coriander Seeds, Garam Masala, Cardamom, Cloves, and more.

🌱 Pulses

Our pulses are sourced from the best farms and processed carefully to maintain their natural nutrition and purity.

Examples:

Chickpeas, Toor Dal, Moong Dal, Urad Dal, Masoor Dal and more.

🍚 Rice

We offer high-quality rice varieties known for their taste, aroma, and long grains.

Examples:

Basmati Rice, Non-Basmati Rice, Sella Rice, Raw Rice and more.

👗 Garments

Along with agro products, we also supply quality garments designed to meet modern fashion and comfort standards.

Examples:

Men’s Wear, Women’s Wear, Casual Wear, Cotton Garments and more.
